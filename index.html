    Jenny's Playtime

    Your friend's little sister has gone missing, so you go to find out. This leads you to a series of events leading to an old dungeon where she still roams. Find a way out of the place. Use your gun to defeat Jenny's army of the dead. Beware of Jenny! Use headphones in full-screen mode for the best experience. Controls: WASD for movement and mouse to look. Click the left mouse to shoot and to pick up boxes. Press 'e' to interact with the environment. Space to jump and ctrl to crawl.
